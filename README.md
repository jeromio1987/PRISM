## Overview
PRISM is an internal analytical framework supporting centrally coordinated purchasing and aftersales revenue intelligence across ISZE distributor markets.

## Contents

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `ISZE_Lubricants_Potential_v1.xlsx` | Lubricants volume potential model by market and segment (v1, April 2026) |

## Model Logic
- Demand = UIO × Oil/Service × 2 services/year
- LCV (D-Max): 6L/service | LD (N-Series): 10L | MD (F-Series): 12.5L
- Volume-only model — no sell-in pricing included in v1
- UIO inputs linked to ISZE_UIO_Overview_2026
- Israel (UTI/UMI) excluded — not willing to change supplier

## Market Scope
- Grand Total potential (excl. Italy): ~2.29M litres/year
- Reachable markets (Approach A): ~1.57M litres/year
- UK (ITUK/IUK) is the largest single market: ~1.0M litres/year
